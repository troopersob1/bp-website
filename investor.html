Our Ecosystem

Three Pillars. One Unified Platform.

SaaS Platform

GoBetter

AI-powered hire-to-retire platform covering the entire frontline workforce lifecycle — hiring, verification, attendance, upskilling, and payroll in one unified system.

Staffing

StaffBetter

End-to-end recruitment and contractual staffing at nationwide scale. Operating as Aasaanjobs in India and MyRobin.id in Indonesia.

Gig Workforce

GigBetter

AI-optimised on-demand workforce deployment. 100% outcome-based with real-time tracking. Operating as TROOPERS in Malaysia and Singapore, and OkayGo in India.
