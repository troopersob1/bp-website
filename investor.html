Think about the people who
keep the world moving.

The delivery rider. The factory worker. The security guard. The gig worker juggling three jobs. Two billion people power the global economy — and for decades, they were the most overlooked, most underserved people in the workforce.

BetterPlace was built to change that. One worker. One enterprise. One better outcome at a time.

The Problem

A Broken System — On Both Sides

The frontline workforce market was broken long before anyone tried to fix it.

For Workers

Invisible. Unverified. Unprotected.

  • No digital identity or verified employment record
  • No access to financial tools or earned wage benefits
  • No clear path for upskilling or career progression
  • Vulnerable to predatory employers with no recourse
For Enterprises

Blind Decisions. Constant Attrition.

  • HR teams drowning in paperwork and manual compliance
  • Compliance nightmares across multiple geographies
  • Attrition that never stops — no retention tools
  • Making workforce decisions blind — no data, no visibility
The Origin

In 2015, Pravin Agarwala sat with a simple conviction:

"Frontline workers deserve the same quality of technology that desk workers take for granted."

Not because it was the charitable thing to do. Because when workers are better managed, better trained, and better protected — enterprises perform better too.

The Platform

Three Pillars. One Unified Platform.

BetterPlace is Asia's leading frontline workforce platform — combining a solutions software layer, staffing operations, and gig workforce services into one integrated ecosystem.

Solutions Platform

The operating system for frontline enterprises. AI-powered, end-to-end — from hire to retire. Background verification. Onboarding. Attendance. Learning. Payroll. Compliance. One platform, total visibility.

Hire Verify Onboard Attend Upskill Payroll
Staffing & Outsourcing

The staffing arm for enterprises who need the right people. Not just bodies — managed, verified, trained workforce solutions with compliance built in. Operating as Aasaanjobs in India and MyRobin.id in Indonesia.

Recruitment Contractual Compliance Nationwide Scale
Gig Workforce

The flexible future — giving workers something rare. Zero commission fees, free training, insurance, and early wage access. 100% outcome-based. Operating as TROOPERS in Malaysia and Singapore, and OkayGo in India.

On-Demand Early Pay Insurance Zero Commission
