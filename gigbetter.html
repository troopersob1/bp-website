How GigBetter Works

AI Matches. Workers Deliver. You Scale.

From requirement to execution in four steps. Our AI engine analyses your needs, builds custom workflows, deploys pre-skilled workers, and tracks outcomes in real time.

Step 1

Analyse Requirements

Share your task type, volume, location, and timeline. We scope the deployment to your exact needs.

Step 2

AI-Powered Matching

Our engine matches pre-screened, rated workers to your tasks based on skill, proximity, and performance history.

Step 3

Deploy & Execute

Workers deployed in under 24 hours. Mobile-based check-in, task tracking, and real-time progress updates.

Step 4

Track & Pay

Real-time analytics dashboard. Automated, outcome-linked payouts. Full transparency at every stage.
