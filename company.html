Our Story

Built to Transform How the World's Largest Workforce Is Managed

"BetterPlace was born when our expertise in creating game-changing technology solutions met our desire to transform the way blue collar employees were perceived." — Pravin Agarwala & Saurabh Tandon, Co-founders

Founded in Bengaluru in 2015, BetterPlace began with a simple belief: frontline workers deserve the same dignity, security, and opportunity as anyone else. A decade later, we're the only platform in Asia that combines AI-powered workforce management software, nationwide staffing, and on-demand gig labour under one roof.

Today, enterprises like Amazon, Shopee, Coca-Cola, and Swiggy trust BetterPlace to manage their most critical asset — their frontline people.
